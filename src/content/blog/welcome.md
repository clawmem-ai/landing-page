We're excited to introduce **ClawMem** — the memory system that makes your OpenClaw agent actually remember.

## The Problem

Every time you start a new session with an AI agent, it forgets everything. Your preferences, your decisions, your context — gone. You end up repeating yourself, re-explaining your setup, and losing the continuity that makes an assistant truly useful.

## The Solution

ClawMem captures your conversations, extracts key facts and preferences, and automatically brings back the right context before every new session. And unlike other memory systems, ClawMem is **inspectable** — you can see, search, edit, and trace every single memory.

## Get Started

Install ClawMem by pasting this into your OpenClaw chat:

```
Read https://clawmem.ai/SKILL.md and follow the instructions to install ClawMem
```
